Privacy Policy

Rymind

Rymind is a reminder and task management app. This page explains what information the app handles, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

Last updated: April 10, 2026
Package: com.avora.planner

1. Information the app handles

Rymind stores reminder titles, notes, task names, reminder dates and times, repeat settings, task state, app appearance settings, and app language settings on the device so the app can function.

If the user enables the optional "Call someone" reminder feature, Rymind opens Android's system contact picker. Rymind does not request broad contacts access. It only receives the contact name and phone number that the user explicitly chooses through the picker.

Rymind does not require account sign-in in the current implementation.

2. How information is used

Rymind uses reminder, task, and contact-selection data only to provide core app features such as:

  • showing reminders and tasks in the app
  • scheduling alerts and lock-screen alarm experiences
  • reopening the phone dialer for call reminders
  • restoring information after device reboot
  • applying selected theme and language preferences

3. Data sharing

Rymind does not sell personal information.

Rymind does not share reminders, tasks, or selected contact information with third parties in the current app implementation.

Rymind does not include advertising SDKs or analytics SDKs in the current app implementation.

4. Data storage and retention

Reminder and task data is stored locally on the device in the app's private storage.

Rymind does not transmit reminder, task, or selected contact data to developer-operated servers in the current app implementation.

Data remains on the device until it is edited, deleted, cleared through app-data removal, or removed by uninstalling the app.

5. Permissions and device capabilities

Rymind may request or rely on:

  • notifications
  • exact alarms
  • full-screen intent
  • wake lock
  • boot completed support

Rymind does not request READ_CONTACTS or CALL_PHONE. Contact selection uses the Android system picker, and calling uses the system dialer.

6. User choices

  • delete reminders and tasks from within the app
  • disable or deny notification and alarm-related access in Android settings
  • clear app data or uninstall the app to remove locally stored data

7. Children's privacy

Rymind is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law in the user's region and is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Policy changes

This policy may be updated to reflect product, legal, or platform changes. The hosted privacy policy URL used in Google Play should always match the latest approved version.
